# NurtureTheory: Tracking Your Child's Health and Developmental Wellbeing

NurtureTheory is a mobile-first pediatric co-pilot that provides parents with a single, intelligent platform to manage health records, triage symptoms, and track developmental milestones. Unlike generic health apps, it utilizes a per-child AI memory layer called Hindsight to provide personalized advice that learns from a parent's own experience over time.

Beyond health management, NurtureTheory empowers parents to actively shape their child’s growth by tracking and logging daily developmental activities and "parenting moments". The platform suggests and monitors key engagements—such as motor skill exercises, social-emotional play, and cognitive challenges—essential for healthy physical and mental development. By synthesizing these observations, the system identifies which activities yield the best outcomes for a specific child, creating a custom playbook for their unique developmental journey.
